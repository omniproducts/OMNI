Game of Thrones

Story

Game of Thrones tells the epic tale of a fractured realm, where noble houses battle for control of the Iron Throne—the seat of ultimate power in the Seven Kingdoms. In the cold North, the honorable Starks fight to protect their people, while in the South, the cunning Lannisters weave webs of politics and betrayal. Across the Narrow Sea, the exiled Daenerys Targaryen rises with three dragons, determined to reclaim the throne stolen from her family. As lords and queens clash, an ancient threat stirs beyond the Wall—an army of the dead that could destroy all of humanity. Alliances shift, heroes fall, and enemies rise in a relentless game where survival depends on wit, courage, and ruthless ambition. In this world, every choice carries a cost, and in the end, you win or you die. imdb rated this tv show 9.2/10

Stranger Things

Story

In the quiet town of Hawkins, Indiana, 1983, something unimaginable stirs in the shadows. When young Will Byers vanishes without a trace, his friends embark on a desperate search, only to encounter a mysterious girl with a shaved head, strange powers, and a number for a name — Eleven. As they uncover her secrets, they realize she’s connected to a hidden world beneath their own — a dark, twisted mirror called the Upside Down, filled with creatures beyond nightmares. Meanwhile, Will’s mother, Joyce, refuses to believe he’s gone, sensing his presence through flickering lights and whispers from the walls. The local police chief, Jim Hopper, digs deeper, unearthing a web of government experiments and cover-ups at Hawkins National Laboratory. The truth is more dangerous than anyone imagined, and the monsters aren’t just in the other world — some walk among them. As the kids, teenagers, and adults of Hawkins unite, they face not only the horrors of the Upside Down but also the trials of friendship, love, and growing up. Each season pulls them into greater peril — shadowy beings, sinister scientists, and a dark force known as the Mind Flayer — testing their courage and their bonds. Stranger Things is a journey into the strange and the supernatural, where loyalty can be the greatest weapon, and even in the darkest night, the light of friendship can fight back the shadows

The Day of The Jackal

Story

A shadow moves across Europe, silent and precise. Known only as the Jackal, he is a man without a past and without a future—only the present mission matters. To the world, he is a phantom assassin: patient, untraceable, and ruthless. But beneath the calm surface lies a dangerous mystery, one that even he cannot fully escape. On the other side stands Bianca Pullman, an MI6 expert with a sharp mind and an unbreakable will. Her life’s pursuit is not glory, but justice, and the Jackal has become her greatest test. Their paths weave through bustling capitals and quiet corners of the continent, each step turning the chase into a deadly game where the stakes are nothing less than survival. Every episode unfolds with new secrets: alliances born in shadows, betrayals waiting in plain sight, and the haunting question of who truly controls the game. The Jackal is no ordinary villain, and Bianca is no ordinary hunter—their duel stretches far beyond bullets and blood. In this modern reimagining of a classic tale, The Day of the Jackal is not just about the kill. It is about obsession, identity, and the thin line between predator and prey. The story refuses to let its audience breathe easily, for the Jackal may strike at any moment, and the hunter may find herself hunted.

Squid Game

Story

Hundreds of desperate players step into a secret game, lured by the promise of wealth beyond imagination. What seems like child’s play quickly turns into a nightmare—every round is simple, every loss is fatal. Among them is Seong Gi-hun, a man broken by debt and failure, who must fight not only for money but for his life. Around him, strangers become allies, allies turn to rivals, and trust is as dangerous as betrayal. Behind the masks, a hidden power watches with cold amusement, pulling the strings of this brutal spectacle. As the games grow bloodier, the question becomes clear: in a world built on greed and survival, who is the real monster—the players, or the ones who made the game?
